How does it work ?
It's IZI, following your needs, choose the theme of your computation :

  • "Basic Formulas" for the basics (all about probabilities!)
  • "Life Insurance Contracts" for contracts in case of life (mainly annuities due either at the beginning or at the end of the year !)
  • "Death Insurance contracts" for contracts in case of death.
  • "Life Plus Death contracts" for contracts combining life and death for example for an Endowment Contract.
You are working with insurrance products and wants a clean and fast website to compute an insurance contract price?
Or you are a clever customer who wants to be aware of pricies before going to negociate a contract with your insurance companiy?
Let us help you solve your problem.
Following the selected contract, you will be asked to give some inputs. Then fill each field and press the compute button.
Everything was made to make you comfortable! For example, for some contracts type, you will be asked to give a value for the interest rate to use. Simply set it one time and IZI Formula Calculator will keep it safe until you finish.
If you get curious or if you want explanations about an izi formula, simply click on the details button. It will give you clean explanations of the aim of the formula and a description of all input and used parameters.
So just click on the button connecting to your need to see what can be computed!

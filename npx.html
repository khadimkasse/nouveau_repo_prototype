npx= lx+n ⁄ lx
where npx is the probability of survival between x and x+n.
the lx are the number of people alive at age x. It can also be viewed this way as the probability of life after n years : you live from age x to age x+1 and from age x+1 to age x+2 and so on until age x+n. Hence it can be computed like : px * px+1 * ... * px+n-1.

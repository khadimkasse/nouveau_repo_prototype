Life Annuity with several payments at the beginning of each period payable up to the death from a fixed age m up to a fixed term n :
mä(k)x:n = mEx*ä(k)x+m:n.
Where k represents the number of payments in the year. For example if k=12, then the annuity is paid monthly. See life annuity due at the beginning of each period for a certain fixed time for more details about ä(k)x+m:n.
See also pure endowment for details about mEx.

Life Annuity with several payments at the end of each period payable up to the death from a fixed age m :
ma(k)x = max + (k-1) ⁄ 2*k *mEx.
Where k represents the number of payments in the year. For example if k=12, then the annuity is paid monthly. See life annuity due at the end of the year, deferred for more details about max.
See also pure endowment for details about mEx.

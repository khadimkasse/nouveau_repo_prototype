Life Annuities :

A life annuity is a contract that pays periodically a certain amount up to the death of the annuitant.
Generally speaking, an annuitant buys a life annuity and makes installment payments for it throughout his working life.
A life annuity is designed to provide a stable income for the annuitant in retirement.

Life Annuity with several payments at the beginning of each period payable up to the death until a fixed term :
ä(k)x:n = äx:n + (k-1) ⁄ 2*k *(1- nEx).
Where k represents the number of payments in the year. For example if k=12, then the annuity is paid monthly. See life annuity payable up to the death at the beginning of the year but for at most n years for more details about äx.
See also pure endowment for details about nEx.

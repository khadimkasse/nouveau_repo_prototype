A pure endowment is a life insurance contract designed to pay a lump sum after a specified term.
The characteristic of the contract are:
- the issue time of the policy : 0,
- benefit : the capital at time n if the insured at time 0 (aged x) is alive.
nEx = (1 ⁄ (1+i)n ) * lx+n ⁄ lx
